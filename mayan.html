Mayan Exhibit at the Nasworthy Museum

Although we have a wide selection of pottery and small sculptures from over a thousand years of Mayan history, perhaps the most fascinating exhibits here are the fine examples of Mayan hieroglyphics. Much more rich in imagination and detail than those of Egypt, the Mayan glyphs appear as intricate cartoons on pottery and paper. In stone, they are ferocious reminders of important rulers and conquests, as well as precise dates. In fact, we can now use the Mayan date glyphs to tell the exact date an event occurred. Don't miss our excellent selection of early writing!
