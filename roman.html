Roman Exhibit at the Nasworthy Museum

The glory of Rome! "To rule mankind and make the world obey," said Virgil, and in our impressive collection of sculpture and artifacts you will catch a glimpse of the might of the empire. Although not as impressive in detail and craftsmanship as their earlier Greek counterparts, the Roman busts and complete sculptures are still remarkable for their lifelike representation of the empire's former inhabitants. Travel back in time to gaze upon long dead emperors, senators and rich wives.
